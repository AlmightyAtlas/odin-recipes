Biscuits & Gravy

These delicious 'Southern Style' Biscuits & Gravy will leave your mouth watering.

If you don't want more after your first plate, well you ain't southern enough partner!

Enough talk hombre... Let's make a breakfast hearty enough for a cowpoke.

What'll need to get started

Ingredients:

  • 2% Milk - 2 Cups
  • Flour - 2 Tblsp
  • Ground Sausage - 8 oz.
  • Canned Biscuits (From Scratch If Preferred)
  • Salt - To Taste
  • Pepper - To Taste

We'll cook this dish on the stovetop, since I'm worried you'd burn it over a fire... SMH

How to prepare:

  1. Cook Sausage on stovetop, breaking up along the way until browned.
  2. Next, stir in the Flour until all the Sausage is covered.
  3. Set the burner to med-high temp and add Milk
  4. Once Milk starts to lightly boil, reduce the burner to low heat.
  5. Cook until the Milk base begins to thicken, remove from heat at preferred thickness.

Eat up... We hope you enjoyed this quick and hearty meal. Now scram!
