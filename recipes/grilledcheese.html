Gooey Grilled Cheese

Grilled Cheese is an American staple. Not loving it feels anti-American. We won't stomach that.

Here's the thing about grilled cheese, it's delicious. It's easy to make and goes great with any soup!

You know the deal with this site, enough of the talk and more of the do!

What you'll need to get started;

Ingredients:

  • 2 Slices Of Your Favorite Cheese
  • 2 Slices Of Bread
  • 1 Tbsp Of Butter

Even the most novice of cooks should be able to handle this one!

How to prepare:

  1. Heat up your favorite frying pan on the stove on med-high heat.
  2. Before the pan gets too hot, add your butter.
  3. Coat one of each side of the bread in the melted butter.
  4. Lay the bread butter-side down in the pan and add the cheese to one side.
  5. Add pieces together on the cheese side
  6. Cook for about 1 minute and flip, cook for an addion 1 minute.
