How to prepare:

  1. Open a can of Campbell's Tomato Soup.
  2. Pour a can of Campbell's Tomato Soup into a pot.
  3. Fill the can with Milk and pour it into the pot.
  4. Heat on med-high for about 5 minutes, reduce heat and simmer.
  5. After another 3 minutes remove from heat. Allow it to cool for 10 minutes.
